# Design System: AI College Study Platform - Digital Sanctuary

## 1. Overview & Creative North Star
**The Creative North Star: "The Digital Sanctuary"**
A high-end, digital sanctuary designed to induce a "flow state" for students through intentional asymmetry, tonal depth, and breathable editorial layouts. Featuring glassmorphism, 3D interactive elements, and nocturnal slate aesthetics.

## 2. Colors & Surface Architecture
- **Base (Surface):** #060e20 (Nocturnal Slate)
- **Primary (Action Indigo):** #a3a6ff
- **Secondary (Growth Emerald):** #69f6b8
- **Surface Container:** #0f1930
- **Glass Effect:** surface-bright with 60% opacity and backdrop-blur (20px-40px).

## 3. Typography
- **Headlines:** Plus Jakarta Sans (Heavy & Authoritative)
- **Body:** Manrope (High legibility)

## 4. 3D Elements (New)
- **Hero Object:** Interactive 3D Crystal/Book using React Three Fiber.
- **Background:** Subtle particle field (Starfield/Fluid simulation).

## 5. Components
- **Buttons:** Indigo gradient, 16px radius, no border.
- **Cards:** No dividers, tonal layering, subtle inner-glow.
- **Focus Shroud:** 80% opacity blur overlay for focus sessions.
